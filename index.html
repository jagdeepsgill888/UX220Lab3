Everything Everywhere All At Once

The recent film that I have watched is "Everything Everywhere All At Once". It is a mind-bending multiverse saga about an immigrant family trying to hold their family together while trying to stop the multiverse from collapsing. I didn't know what the film was about, but I heard it was a great film as it won a lot of awards. I was immediately able to recognize Michelle Yeoh because I grew up watching her Cantonese movies from Hong Kong before she made it to Hollywood. They did a character switch when they cast Michelle and made her the leading role after Jackie Chan turned down the film.

The film turned out to be great at the end. It was a visually stunning film packed with action, dynamic fight scenes, visual effects, and many creative takes. It also touched on a lot of issues that are often left unacknowledged in immigrant households with a dysfunctional family structure where they were not communicating what was actually bothering them or was on their mind. I highly recommend this film if you haven't seen it yet!
